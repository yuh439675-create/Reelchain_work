## 2. How to Register and Log In

### 2.1 Download the App
First, you need to download the short drama app to your phone:

- **Apple Phones**: Search for the app name in the App Store and download it.

- **Android Phones**: Install via the provided download link.

### 2.2 Register an Account

The app supports multiple registration methods; choose the most convenient one:

#### Method 1: Register with Mobile Number (Recommended)

1. Open the app and click the "Login/Register" button.

2. Enter your **mobile phone number**.

3. Click "Get Verification Code," and the system will send a verification code to your mobile phone via SMS.

4. Enter the received **verification code**.

5. Set a **login password** (for future logins).

6. Click "Register" to complete.

#### Method Two: Email Registration

1. Select "Email Registration"

2. Enter your **email address**.

3. Click "Get Verification Code" and check your email for the verification code.

4. Enter the verification code and set a password.

5. Click "Register" to complete.

#### Method Three: Third-Party Account Login (Fastest)

Supports the following methods for quick login:

- **Apple Account Login** (Apple ID)

- **Google Account Login** (Google)

- **Facebook Account Login**

- **WeChat Account Login**

> üí° **Tip**: After logging in with a third-party account, the system will require you to bind your mobile phone number for enhanced security.

### 2.3 Enter Invitation Code (Optional) If a friend sent you an invitation code:

- During registration, find the "Invitation Code" input box

- Enter your friend's invitation code

- This way, your friend can receive a referral reward

### 2.4 Login Account After successful registration, when you open the app in the future:

- Enter your **phone number/email** + **password** to log in

- Or directly use a third-party account for one-click login

### 3.2 Steps to Watch Short Dramas

#### Step 1: Select a Short Drama

1. Find your favorite short drama on the homepage

- Click on a specific episode, such as "Episode 1"

#### Free Viewing:

- The first few episodes of each short series are **free** (usually the first 3-5 episodes).

- You can watch the free episodes first, and decide whether to pay if you like them.

#### Unlock by Watching Ads:

- Some episodes can be **unlocked for free by watching ads**.

- After watching a 15-30 second ad, the episode can be watched for free.

#### Paid Viewing: Later episodes require payment, and there are two ways:

1. **Purchase a Membership**: Members can watch all short series for free (recommended!)

2. **Unlock with Coins**: Purchase a single episode.

## 6. How to Invite Friends to Earn Money

This is a **share to earn money** feature. Invite friends to register and recharge, and you will receive a commission.

#### Level 1 Invitation (Directly Invited Friends)

- Friend's successful registration: Reward X yuan

- Friend's first recharge: You receive X% of the recharge amount

- Subsequent recharges by the friend: You continue to receive X% commission

#### Level 2 Invitation (Friends Invited by Friends)

- Your friend then invites others

- You also receive a level 2 commission (lower percentage)

- Fill in your invitation code during registration.

#### Step 4: Receive Rewards

- You receive a registration reward when your friend successfully registers.

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: What should I do if I don't receive the verification code during registration?

**A:**

- Check if your phone number is entered correctly.

- Check if your SMS has been blocked (see the "Spam" folder).

- Wait 1-2 minutes; the verification code may be delayed.

- Try registering with your email address.

- Contact customer service for assistance.

### Q2: Do I still need coins after purchasing a membership?

**A:**

- No! Members can watch all short dramas for free.

- Choose either coins or membership.

- Recommendation: Membership is more cost-effective for frequent short drama viewers.

### Q3: How do I continue watching a short drama if I'm halfway through?

**A:**

- The system automatically records your viewing progress.

- The next time you open the short drama, it will resume playback from where you left off.

- Or go to the "Following Dramas" page and find the short drama.

### Q4: How do I save my favorite short dramas? **A:**

1. Go to the short drama details page.

2. Click the "Favorite" button (‚≠ê icon).

3. You can view your favorite short dramas in "My ‚Üí Favorites".

### Q5: What should I do if a short drama won't play/plays failed?

**A:**

- Check your network connection (Wi-Fi is recommended).

- Switch the resolution (click on player settings).

- Restart the app and try again.

- Update the app to the latest version.

- Contact customer service for feedback.

### Q6: My invited friend hasn't bound my invitation code?

**A:**

- Your friend needs to fill in the invitation code during registration.

- If you forgot to fill it in during registration:

1. Go to "My ‚Üí Settings".

2. Find "Bind Inviter".

3. Enter your invitation code.

4. Submit the binding.

### Q7: How do I contact customer service? **A:**

1. Go to the "My" page.

2. Click "Help Center" or "Online Customer Service".

3. You can view frequently asked questions or ask questions online.

4. You can also contact customer service via Telegram (the customer service link is in the app settings).
